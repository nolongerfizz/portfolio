~$ About me

My journey into tech began the day my father brought home our first computer. What started as curiosity quickly became an obsession — I spent hours exploring the internet, breaking things (in the good way), and trying to understand how it all worked. Shows like Mr. Robot didn't help — they just pushed me deeper down the rabbit hole.

I taught myself how to code by digging through online resources, building small projects, and messing up (a lot). Eventually, I enrolled in CS50x, Harvard's Introduction to Computer Science, to strengthen my foundation. Now, I'm sharpening my skills even further in a Full Stack Java Developer Bootcamp at Generation.
